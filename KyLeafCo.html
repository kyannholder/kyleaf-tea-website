About KyLeaf & Co.

Founded by Kyann Holder, KyLeaf & Co. is a premium tea company that brings the finest blends to your cup. With a passion for high-quality, organic teas, our mission is to offer a unique and unforgettable tea experience. From Organic Green Tea to Classic Black, we ensure that every tea leaves a lasting impression.

Meet the Founder & CEO

Kyann Holder, the Founder and CEO of KyLeaf & Co., is an entrepreneur with a deep love for tea and sustainability. Kyann’s vision for KyLeaf & Co. is to bring quality, ethical tea to tea lovers around the world while promoting environmental sustainability and responsible sourcing.

Our History

KyLeaf & Co. began with a passion for quality teas. After years of exploring the best tea plantations and learning about the intricate art of tea making, Kyann Holder decided to create a company that would bring the finest tea experience to customers globally. What started as a small venture in the Caribbean has now become a recognized name in the world of specialty teas.

Our Mission

At KyLeaf & Co., our mission is simple: to bring you the finest tea blends, crafted with love and care. We strive to create a culture of sustainability, innovation, and excellence in everything we do. Whether you're enjoying a cup of our Organic Green Tea or one of our specialty blends, our goal is to make every sip an experience.
